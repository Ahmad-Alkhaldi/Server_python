Welcome to our course Computer Networks!


Upon the successful completion of this course a student should
understand the computer Networking concepts, basic terminology, and
applications. In addition, the students will understand the Internet
architecture,components, services, and measures of performance.
Furthermore, the application-layer,transport-layer,network-layer concepts
and routing principles, data link-layer concepts, protocols, and services
such as error-detection and correction, addressing, and multiple-access
techniques will be discussed.
